If you're flying to the Soča Valley, **Trieste is your airport**. Trieste Airport (Ronchi dei Legionari, code **TRS**) sits just across the Italian border and is the **closest airport to the valley** — closer and usually calmer than Venice, Ljubljana or Klagenfurt.

## How long is the drive?

- **Trieste Airport → Kobarid:** about **1 hour 10 minutes** (~63 km).
- **Trieste Airport → Bovec:** about **1 hour 30 minutes** (~84 km).

The route runs from the airport toward **Cividale del Friuli**, crosses into Slovenia, and follows the Soča River up the valley — a genuinely scenic approach, not just a transfer.

## The border

You'll cross from Italy into Slovenia, but **both countries are in the Schengen Area**, so there are normally **no routine passport checks** — you just drive through. Keep ID handy regardless.

## Flying in and out of Trieste — base smart

If you're bookending the trip with Trieste (fly in, fly out), pick a base that keeps both the airport run and the valley easy:

- **Kobarid** is the sweet spot — about **1h10 from the airport**, central in the valley, with great food and the headline sights (Kozjak Waterfall, the Napoleon Bridge, the WWI museum) on the doorstep. Both ends of the valley are short day trips, and the drive back to Trieste on departure day is straightforward.
- **Bovec** suits you if the trip is adventure-led (rafting, the high roads) — just budget the extra ~20 minutes each way to and from the airport.

## Rent a car

You'll want **your own car**. Public transport from Trieste into the valley is slow and indirect, and the waterfalls, gorges and WWI sites sit off the main road. Pick up a rental **at Trieste Airport** and drop it back there before your flight out — simplest for a round trip.

## A sensible round-trip shape

1. **Land at Trieste**, collect the car, drive to **Kobarid** (~1h10).
2. **Explore the valley** from there — Kobarid and Tolmin one way, Bovec and the high roads the other.
3. **Drive back to Trieste** the evening before, or the morning of, your flight — allow buffer for the mountain roads and the airport.
